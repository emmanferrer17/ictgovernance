Introduction

The NBI-CCD is the specialized investigative unit of the National Bureau of Investigation.

It handles complex, high-profile cybercrime cases involving national security, organized crime, and transnational digital threats, working as the premier technical investigative arm.

Key Responsibilities
1
Technical Investigations

Conduct technical investigations into hacking, identity theft, and data interference.

2
Forensic Analysis

Perform forensic data recovery and analysis on seized electronic devices.

3
International Collaboration

Collaborate with international bodies like INTERPOL to track cross-border cybercriminals.

4
Technical Assistance

Provide technical assistance to the CICC (Cybercrime Investigation and Coordination Center).
